About Mike Richardson

Mike Richardson is an avid hunter and fisherman who has done both his entire life. Years of experience on the water and in the field shaped the approach behind RCS Guide Services. From chasing fish to preparing hunting land, stands, blinds, and food plots, Mike is passionate about helping others enjoy the outdoors the right way.
